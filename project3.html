Microprocessor-based Energy Saving Clock.

Embedded C for Microcontrollers, Mini Individual Project (Imperial College London)

Project Overview

The main aim of this project was to design and program a device that responds to ambient light levels, and switched off during small hours of the morning (between 1am and 5am). It should also be able to display the current hour of the day on the LED array in binary, adjust for daylight savings time, be fully automatic and maintain synchronicity with the sun indefinitely.
